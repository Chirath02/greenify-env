Illegal felling of sandalwood trees rampant in Attapadi

PALAKKAD: The illegal felling of sandalwood trees from different parts of Pudur and Sholayur grama panchayats had been reported from Attappadi Hills during the last couple of weeks.

There were incidents of felling of old trees and also planted sandalwood of 20 to 30 years old from Veettikundu, Puliyappathi, Kadampara and Marappalam in Sholayur grama panchayat and Mulli, Cheerakkadavu and Bommiyampadi in Pudur grama panchayat.

An old sandalwood tree was felled from the house compound of a political leader at Agali town last week. Two days back, its roots were digged out and taken, said M Sukumaran, convenor, Attappady Samrakshana Samithy.
